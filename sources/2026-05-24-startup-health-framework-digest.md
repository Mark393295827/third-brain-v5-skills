## Skill-Relevant Synthesis

- Startup health should not be judged only by a pitch or a 24-step checklist. It needs a stage-aware health diagnosis across opportunity, team, resources, PMF, capital, and risk.
- The entrepreneurship map contributes the lifecycle: opportunity recognition, product premise validation, PMF, team building, financing, execution, and exit.
- The VC map contributes the 5T lens: Team, Target Market, Tech/Product, Traction, and Terms, plus power-law upside and TOPSCAN-style operating support.
- The efficiency map contributes the operating method: define the question, use MECE decomposition, separate assumptions from facts, and test the cheapest falsifiable hypothesis.
- The investor canvas contributes market-cycle awareness: AI, defense tech, fintech, private credit, regional shifts, valuation gaps, macro/geopolitical risks, IPO pipeline, and fundraising/exit conditions.

## Repo Translation

- `startup-evaluation` now outputs a startup health memo, not only a general startup assessment.
- It scores customer pain, market timing, 10x value, PMF/traction, unit economics, team/governance, capital/runway, and moat/risk.
- It separates VC-scale attractiveness from bootstrapped business health.
- It adds evidence ladder, runway thresholds, AI/hard-tech addendum, top-constraint diagnosis, and next-cheapest-test design.
